About Vamos Collective

A student-founded initiative for the parts of university learning that feel underserved.

Vamos Collective was created in response to the challenges of independent university learning in Singapore. Students often feel lost, academic support at the university level is underserved, and peer learning can be powerful when it is thoughtful, screened, and human.
