The University Learning Gap

University learning is often more independent and less guided than students expect.

Large classes, limited personalised guidance, and the expectation of self-directed learning can leave students unsure of how to study effectively. Sometimes, lectures, notes, and self-study simply aren't enough. That's where Vamos Collective comes in.

Large classes

It's easy to fall behind when hundreds of students are moving through the same material.

Limited feedback

Students often need sharper feedback than office hours or group chats can provide.

Self-directed learning

Knowing what to prioritise can be as difficult as the content itself.

Study methods

Effective university study looks different across modules, assessments, and faculties.
